We are constantly bombarded with the latest trends and programs for exercising. Across the board, there is a fixation with maximizing the number of calories burned. In reality, the intended objective is usually to burn that pesky fat. What is often unrecognizable, are the different “zones” of training. Here, we can take note from arguably the population with the most prominent mitochondrial density and metabolic health — endurance athletes.
